The Best Movies According to Karim Menem

My Top 3 Movies of all-Time:.

Rush Hour Franchise

A trio of extremely funny and exciting movies starring Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker.
Personally I think the choice of these actors is the perfect match for these movies.

Despicable Me

My favorite Animated Movie. I can watch it 1000 times consecutively and
honestly I don't think I'd get bored of it.

Avengers EndGame

No words needed to describe the greatness of this movie as it is known world wide.
Even though I did not experience it in the cinema when it released I still enjoyed it to the fullest.
